中药熏蒸治疗小儿痉挛型脑瘫疗效观察

发表时间:[2015-4-8 10:34:00]

中药熏蒸治疗小儿痉挛型脑瘫疗效观察

黄霞 陈星 王臣 汪文林 鲁晓林

浙江民政康复中心 杭州 310016

摘要: [目的] 观察中药熏蒸结合运动疗法治疗痉挛型脑瘫的疗效。[方法] 年龄在6个月-6岁的60例痉挛型脑瘫患儿，随机将他们分为治疗组和对照组。治疗组采用中药熏蒸结合运动疗法，对照组只采用运动疗法，两组均采用每周5次治疗，3个月为一疗程，疗程结束后进行评价疗效。评价采用改良Ashworth和粗大运动功能量表。统计学分析采用 SPSS18.0软件，计量资料采用配对t检验。[结果] 两组患儿治疗后痉挛评分较治疗前均有所降低, GMFM-88分值也均有提高，结果有统计学意义( P<0.05) , 提示对照组及治疗组两组均能缓解患儿痉挛状态，提高患儿的运动功能。两组治疗后痉挛评分和 GMFM-88分值比较有统计学意义( P<0.05)，提示两组治疗对痉挛评分和运动功能的影响具有差异性。[结论] 在提高痉挛型脑瘫的运动功能，缓解肌痉挛方面，中药熏蒸结合运动疗法优于单纯的运动疗法。

关键词 脑性瘫痪 中药熏蒸 康复

Effect of Chinese Medicine Fumigation on Children with Spastic Cerebral Palsy. HUNG Xia, CHEN Xing,WANG Chen,WANG Wen-lin,LU Xiao-lin, Rehabilitation Centre of the ZheJiang Civil Affairs Bureau , HangZhou(310016), China Abstract： [Objective］To examine the effect of Chinese Medicine Fumigation on children with spastic cerebral palsy .［Methods］ In this study, 60 children with spastic cerebral palsy whose ages were 6 months - 6 years were randomly divided into experimental group and control group. The experimental group used herbal fumigation treatment with physical therapy，the control group used physical therapy，five times a week，three months for one period of treatment.Treatment effects were evaluated using the Modified Ashworth Scale(MAS) and the Gross Motor Function Measure(GMFM-88) scale before and after therapy. It used SPSS18.0 statistical package and paired－samples t test.［Result］ MAS and GMFM-88 scores of both groups after therapy were significantly different(P＜0.05). The experimental group was superior to the control group(P＜0.05).［Conclusion］ Herbal fumigation treatment with physical therapy was superior to physical therapy alone.

Keywords： cerebral palsy;herbal fumigation;rehabilitation
